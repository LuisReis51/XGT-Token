# How to Buy and Mine XGT Token - Complete Guide

## Prerequisites
1. Install MetaMask: https://metamask.io/download/
2. Get some BNB (Binance Coin) for transactions

## Setting Up Binance Smart Chain (BSC)
1. Open MetaMask
2. Click the network dropdown (usually shows "Ethereum Mainnet")
3. Click "Add Network"
4. Add BSC with these details:
   - Network Name: `Binance Smart Chain`
   - RPC URL: `https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/`
   - Chain ID: `56`
   - Symbol: `BNB`
   - Block Explorer: `https://bscscan.com`

## Getting BNB
1. Option 1: Buy directly through MetaMask
   - Click "Buy" in MetaMask
   - Choose your payment method
   - Purchase BNB

2. Option 2: Through Binance
   - Buy BNB on Binance
   - Withdraw to your MetaMask wallet
   - Make sure to use BSC network for withdrawal

## Buying XGT on PancakeSwap
1. Go to PancakeSwap: https://pancakeswap.finance/swap?outputCurrency=0x654E38A4516F5476D723D770382A5EaF8Bae0e0D
2. Connect your wallet (click "Connect Wallet")
3. Enter the amount of BNB you want to spend
4. Click "Swap"
5. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask

## Adding XGT to MetaMask
1. Open MetaMask
2. Click "Import tokens"
3. Enter the contract address: `0x654E38A4516F5476D723D770382A5EaF8Bae0e0D`
4. Click "Add Custom Token"
5. Click "Import"

## Mining XGT
1. Go to the mining interface: http://localhost:8000/mining
2. Connect your wallet
3. Make sure you're on BSC network
4. Click "Start Mining"
5. Approve the transaction in MetaMask
6. Wait for confirmation
7. Your mined XGT will appear in your wallet

## Providing Liquidity (Optional)
1. Go to: https://pancakeswap.finance/add/BNB/0x654E38A4516F5476D723D770382A5EaF8Bae0e0D
2. Connect your wallet
3. Enter the amount of BNB you want to provide
4. Enter the corresponding amount of XGT
5. Click "Supply"
6. Confirm the transaction

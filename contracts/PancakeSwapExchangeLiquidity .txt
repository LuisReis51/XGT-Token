First, go to: https://pancakeswap.finance/add/BNB/0x654E38A4516F5476D723D770382A5EaF8Bae0e0D
Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right corner and select MetaMask
You'll see two input fields:
First field: BNB (set this to 0.05)
Second field: XGT (set this to 400,000,000)
Before clicking "Supply", make sure you have:
At least 0.05 BNB in your wallet
Approved XGT tokens for PancakeSwap (it will prompt you to approve if needed)
Click "Supply" and confirm the transaction in MetaMask
After the transaction is confirmed, the price will be set and our mining interface should start showing the price automatically
